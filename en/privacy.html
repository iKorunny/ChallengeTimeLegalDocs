Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing ChallengeTime. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle permissions and data within the app.

2. Access Permissions

  • Notifications: We request permission to send reminders for your active challenges. This helps you stay on track and achieve your goals.
  • Photo Library: Access is required only to allow you to personalize your challenges with custom images. Your photos stay on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

Our app offers Premium features via auto-renewable subscriptions. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your credit card details. We only receive information about your subscription status to unlock premium content.

4. Advertising and Analytics

We use Yandex Advertising Network to display ads, which allows us to keep the app free. Yandex may collect anonymous identifiers (IDFA) and analytics to show relevant ads and improve service quality.

5. Data Protection

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. All data processed is used strictly for app functionality.
